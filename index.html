Luis Luque

Email Developer
View projects

Hello there! I’m Luis, an email developer that specializes in creating good-looking and responsive emails that work on all kinds of screen sizes and email clients—including the most outdated ones (ahem, Outlook). On the marketing side, I have experience with Salesforce Marketing Cloud and I’m familiar with setting up and optimizing automations, using AMPscript to personalize emails, and examining Key Performance Indicators to understand the effectiveness of a campaign.

Other than being an email nerd, I enjoy hiking and playing chess in my spare time. The latter not only speaks to my analytical skills and attention to detail, but also reflects my love for challenges. Just as I analyze the position on the board to find the best move, I bring the same focus and determination to my work in email development. No matter the challenge, it’s this combination of attention to detail and strategic problem-solving that drives my approach to creating engaging and effective email campaigns.

While I do not yet have formal experience in the industry, I’ve been honing my skills through personal projects, and continuous learning about everything email. That being said, I’m super excited to bring my knowledge to a professional environment and contribute effectively to your team.
